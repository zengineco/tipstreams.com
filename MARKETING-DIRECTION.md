# TipStreams Marketing Direction

## One-Line Positioning

TipStreams is the fastest way for a streamer to turn wallets and payment links they already own into a clean QR page and OBS overlay.

## The Flagship Claim

Use this:

> Create a branded tip QR in 10 seconds.

But qualify it nearby:

> Already have a wallet or payment link? Paste it, brand it, download it.

Do not imply TipStreams creates payment accounts, processes tips, or replaces Streamlabs. It does not. That honesty is a strength.

## Primary Audience

Start with small and mid-sized streamers who already care about direct support:

- crypto-friendly Twitch, YouTube, and Kick streamers
- IRL streamers who can show or print a QR
- music/podcast livestreamers
- creators with scattered payment links
- creators who dislike platform cuts or bloated donation tools

Do not start with enterprise creators, agencies, or people who need tax reporting, refund tooling, donor CRM, or managed payouts.

## Core Message

Your viewers should not need to hunt for support links.

TipStreams gives you:

- one QR for a wallet, payment app, or permanent tip page
- one page for multiple payment options
- one OBS overlay that makes support visible on stream
- zero payment custody, zero platform cut from TipStreams

## What To Lead With

Lead with the smallest useful action:

1. Paste a wallet or payment link.
2. Brand the QR.
3. Put it on stream.

Then graduate users:

1. Claim permanent page.
2. Add two or three methods.
3. Export overlay.
4. Add to OBS.

## What Not To Lead With

Do not lead with:

- automation gimmicks
- blockchain alert engines
- event runtimes
- marketplaces
- DogeFundMe
- platform architecture

Those may matter later. Right now they distract from the product's useful first action.

## First 30 Days

Week 1: Make five short demo clips.

- "Make a BTC tip QR in 10 seconds"
- "Add a permanent tip QR to OBS"
- "One QR for PayPal, Cash App, Venmo, and crypto"
- "How to test your tip QR before going live"
- "Why TipStreams does not touch your money"

Week 2: Post where streamers already ask setup questions.

- OBS forums and subreddits
- Twitch/Kick creator Discords
- crypto creator groups
- small streamer communities
- YouTube comments on OBS setup tutorials, only where useful

Week 3: Direct outreach.

Message 50 creators who already show donation links, crypto wallets, or Cash App tags. Offer to make their first overlay free.

Week 4: Turn replies into proof.

Collect screenshots, friction points, and exact phrases users use. Put the best phrases back into the homepage.

## Outreach DM

Hey - quick useful thing. I built TipStreams so streamers can turn a wallet or payment link into a branded QR and OBS overlay without routing money through another platform.

If you already use PayPal, Cash App, Venmo, Ko-fi, or crypto, it lets viewers scan one QR and support you directly.

I can make your first overlay if you want to see how it looks on your stream.

https://tipstreams.com

## Cutoffs

Build now:

- clearer onboarding
- better overlay instructions
- mobile scan testing copy
- 2-3 strong templates
- simple demo videos

Build later:

- live alerts
- blockchain polling
- event overlays
- template marketplace
- creator accounts beyond what permanent pages need

Ignore for now:

- generic growth-agent features
- complicated dashboards
- anything that makes TipStreams look like it processes payments
